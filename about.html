About Me

My Story

Hi there, I'm John. Some of you may know me as The Debug Life, but I have not always been involved in the tech/programming space. I previously graduated with a bachelor of Medical Science with Honours. I had a thesis that assessed the influence of a novel cannabinoid receptor agonist drug in Parkinson's disease modelled mice.

Before starting this journey of learning programming and studying computer Science, I was working for the Medical Oncology Clinical Trials Unit at Westmead hospital. A few months in the job, I felt burnt out to some degree. One evening on way back home, I asked myself a question "Am I going to do this for the rest of my life?" I told myself no. I soon realised that, emotional investment is the key to fulfilment and enjoyment in any job.

At the time, everything that I worked for felt like it was a waste and I regretted not making these decisions earlier. It too me some time to process what I had realised before I took any action. But here in 2025, I have learned so much about programming and learning new skills as I progress through my degree.

My Journey

I am currently studying a computer science degree, undertaking a Google Cybersecurity course , and learning how to design a website using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

As a mature aged student, I found it quite difficult to make the decision to go back to studying in university. There were many challenges that I have encountered during my time studying. I was learning new things and concepts, which changed my learning approaches. It took me quite some time to learn, adjust and adapt to how a programmer would learn. The lesson that I learned in 2024 was applying concepts in projects and assessing your code.

My Goal

My goal is to land a Cybersecurity analyst job in either defensive (blue team) or offensive (red team) security. However, an alternative is to land a web development role. I enjoyed the process of making my own portfolio website, so I would like to make more websites as I progress further into my career aspirations.

My secondary goals are to establish a personal brand through content creation and contribute in a proactive manner to people who are in the same situation as I am, going through the same struggles as I am. I want to help people understand programming and get better at it.
